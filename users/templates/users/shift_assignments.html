💡 How Shifts Work
  • Employees with an assigned shift will follow that shift's schedule
  • Employees without an assigned shift will follow the company's Fixed Schedule
  • If your company uses Shift Schedule, you must assign shifts to employees
  • Late and early departure calculations are based on the assigned shift times
  • You can change an employee's shift at any time using the dropdown above
